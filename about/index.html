Lost Decade Games is an award-winning independent game company founded by Geoff Blair and Matt Hackett in 2010. Their first game Onslaught! Arena established them as early pioneers of HTML5 gaming. With their tactical strategy RPG Lava Blade and castle defense shoot 'em up Rampart Rush, Lost Decade Games aims to create great experiences for gamers with a passion for solid gameplay and carefully crafted game designs.
