Lost Decade Games is an award-winning independent game company founded by Geoff Blair and Matt Hackett in 2010. Their first game Onslaught! Arena established them as early pioneers of HTML5 gaming.

With their Kickstarter-funded action RPG A Wizard's Lizard, Lost Decade Games aims to create great experiences for gamers with a passion for solid gameplay and carefully crafted game designs.
