Privacy Policy

We promise that:

  • we will never spam you…
  • we will never disclose your personal information without your permission (unless required by law)…
  • we will protect your information from other users.

We may sell, rent or share information about user habits, but only in anonymous aggregate. We will never do so in a manner that could even remotely be individually identifiable. For example, we might aggregate data like, "out of 1,000 players, 25% played for 5 minutes," but wouldn't expose any individual person's specific data.
