Onslaught! is the spiritual successor to Onslaught! Arena. Originally a pure HTML5 game, Onslaught! is now available on iPad thanks to the Game Closure SDK.

The basic gameplay mechanics, creature behaviors and everything else from Onslaught! Arena is still here, but we also added new enemies, new environments and a store with awesome gear for your playing pleasure. While it's not quite a sequel, we wanted to add enough new content to be worth a look on these new mobile platforms.
